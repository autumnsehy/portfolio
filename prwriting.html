BUILD FAYETTEVILLES FUTURE LIBRARY CAMPAIGN


As an intern at Company Politics, my job was mostly to write copy and organize voter data for a campaign to expand the Fayetteville Public Library.

I was the campaign go-to copy writer. On our website I write this page, this page, this page and this page.

In the few final weeks of the campaign I also wrote emails sent to over 15,000 people- pictures of them are at the bottom of this post. This was one of the largest email campaigns ever done at Company Politics.

We ended up winning the vote- there were two measures we needed passed. The first won 3,617 votes to 2,550 and the second 3,439 votes to 2,726.
