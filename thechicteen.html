The Chic Teen Website



What's left of the site


These are some images I have saved from my fashion blog thechicteen.com.

Unfortunately I couldn't afford to maintain the website (student budget) after I accidentally murdered it while trying to change the site address.

I ran the website from the spring of 2012 to the fall of 2016. In its heyday, summer of 2014 I would reach about 100 people per day. I was featured three times in the now defunct Independent Fashion Bloggers featured posts of the week.

I used Wordpress to maintain the website and learned how to code in PHP, HTML and CSS. One of my hardest projects was creating the scrolling Twitter feed- I learned some Javascript to do that.

I'm still learning how to create slideshows- so please go to this corresponding Weebly page to view screenshots of my old site.
