Languages



Salut! J'aime étudier les langues étrangère. Je parle le français plus bien que les autres langues j'étudié, mais je ne suis pas couramment.

Det är svårt att bestämma vad är flytande. Är man flyt om man kan prata med ett barn eller om man kan lasa en historik bok?

انا لا اعرِف

"Hi! I like studying foreign languages. I speak French better then the other languages I study- but I am not fluent.
It is hard to decide what fluency is. Are you fluent if you can talk to a child or if you can read a history book? I don't know." (French, Swedish and Arabic)

Using CEFR levels, I consider myself B2 (upper intermediate) in French, B1 (lower intermediate) in Swedish, and A1 or A2 (beginner) in Arabic. My goal are to reach C1 in French, C2 in Swedish and B2 in Arabic- the Swedish is higher because my boyfriend is Swedish.
