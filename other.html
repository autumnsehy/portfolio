Other websites



My work with websites began as a high school honors project. Since that project, I've worked on many more sites and have learned basics of coding. Lots of those sites have been updated since then- so this section is for the work I've done but is now lost into the internet oblivion.

The two websites I did the most work on were: tigertimesonline.com and stackingbenjamins.com. From these websites I learned how to edit high-traffic pages, create fun effects like scrolling twitter feeds and work around Wordpress's sometimes frustrating structure. I also learned how to make 404 pages, RSS feeds and how to filter spam.
