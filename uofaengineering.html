UofA Engineering Web Internship



For this internship I convert old staff websites for the engineering department to new Wordpress sites. Sometimes I also teach staff how to update their site.

I'm able to convert about two websitse a week, depending on how complicated the website is. Some things I've learned include: how to make one-page sites, how to make interesting design changes from text and how to convert a lot of data from one site to another.
